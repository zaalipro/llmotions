How It Works

From a sentence to a living motion in three steps

01

Describe It

Type what you want — as casually or precisely as you like. "A person walks confidently across the stage, pauses, and turns to wave." That's all it takes.

02

LLMotions Understands

Our multimodal LLM deconstructs intent, infers timing, body mechanics, and emotional register — then encodes everything into high-fidelity motion tokens.
